Haram Snacks

General Info about Haram Items:

  1. Gelatin and Jelly - If contains only the word 쇼트닝 or 동물성유 then avoid it. If contains 쇼트닝(대무) then its Halal.

  2. Cheese/ Cheese products- Cheese is prepared by using Rennet (obtained from the stomach of animals, specially in Korea pig is the source).

  3. Marshmallow - usually sticky content It contains Gelatin.

  4. Whey/Whey powder - Like in making cheese, the rennet is used as an enzyme to make whey.

  5. Vanilla Flavor/extract 바닐라 - The flavor extracted from vanilla beans is most commonly dissolved in alcohol. Documentation from a distributor of vanilla extract showed that the ingredient contained 50% alcohol.
