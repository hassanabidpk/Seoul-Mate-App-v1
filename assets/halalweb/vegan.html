Vegan Food in Korea

Korea is country for meat-lovers, however it has started accommodating Vegans. Below you can find some info related to Vegan food

  1. Alien's Day Out Blog. A Vegan who writes all abot being vegetarian/vegan in Seoul and in Korea. She also bakes delicious vegan desserts and sells them to cafes all around Seoul. Link to Blog

  2. Loving Hut Restaurants. chain of vegan restaurants in Korea and around the world. There are several in Seoul. Loving Hut Website

  3. Garobee Restaurant. Vegan buffet in Gangnam, between Gangnam and Sinnonhyeon Stations. There is a review of Garobee with pictures on Alien's Day Out.

  4. Food Stores for Vegans and Vegeterian. Foreign Food Mart and High Street Market (Itaewon). High Street Market sells hummus, homemade pesto, and this wonderful vegan cheese made from cashews. HighStreet Market Facebook Page

  5. iHerb.com online store. They have over 30,000 products and they ship to Korea VERY cheap. iHerb Webpage
