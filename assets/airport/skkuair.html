SungKyungKwan University Airport-to-Campus Route(Seoul campus)

Details :

  1. Platform: 5B 12A

  2. Bus No: 6011

  3. Bus Fare: 10,000 Won

  4. Stop name: SungKyungKwan University

  5. How to get to University from Stop: After getting off at the stop, cross the road and turn right at the junction you can see Dunkin Donuts and walk for about 5 minutes. You will see the university on your left
