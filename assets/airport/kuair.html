Korea University Airport-to-Campus Route(Seoul campus)

Details :

  1. Platform: 3B 10A

  2. Bus No: 6101

  3. Bus Fare: 14,000 Won

  4. Stop name: Korea University Station

  5. How to get to University from Stop: After getting off at the stop cross the road and walk for about 5 minutes, you will see the university at your right which is the other side of road
