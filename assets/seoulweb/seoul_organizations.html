Student Organizations in Seoul

While attending a university at Seoul, students can participate in various organizations as listed below

  1. Seoul International Students Forum (SISF). It is orchestered by Seoul Global Center under umbrella of Seoul Metropolitan Government. SISF Website Link

    Please note that SISF was not organized in 2013, so please check Seoul Global Center Website for updates. Seoul Global Center

  2. Korea-International Student Support Association (KISSA). It is non-profit organization KISSA Website Link

  3. Korea International Student Association (KINSA). KINSA Website Link

  4. International Student Fellowship(ISF). ISF is a Christian organization that aims to welcome, befriend, and offer hospitality to international students from all over the world. ISF Website Link
