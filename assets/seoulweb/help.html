How to use this app :

There are five main sections in this App. They are described shortly as below:

  1. Campus Maps.

    2. This section contains maps of universities with share feature. You can see campus map and find out any department or student center easily

    Using Facebook and Kakao share feature you can tell your friends about campus maps weblink (to iPhone or PC users)

  2. Google Maps.

    3. This section contains customized google maps of universities, which are created with help of local university students. As google and naver maps show names of places in and around university in Korea, this customized map marks important places around university using English.

    For example : It tells you nearest bus stop and subway station. It also tells you location of dormitory, post office, banks etc.

    Same with Campus maps, you can share this map with your friends using Facebook or Kakao share feature. You can also clear or reset markers

  3. Airport Bus

    4. This section gives information about Airport Bus that you should take from Incheon airport towards your university. Platform no, bus fare and final destination stop name are explicitly mentioned. You can also share this info. with you friends

  4. Special Dietaries.

    5. This section contains bried information about Vegan, Vegetarian and Halal food.

    At this point of time, Halal food section is thoroughly developed with halal of Muslim community especially Pakistanis and Malaysians.

    Corresponding restaurant locations are provided in Google Maps. They can be easily reached out.

  5. Seoul Life.

    6. This section contains general information about how to settle in Seoul after arrival.

    This is sub-divided in various sections. You can get Seoul Global Centers Locations and also real-estate shops who supports English

    News and Events section will give you updated information about Travel, Volunteering and Korean Language studies
